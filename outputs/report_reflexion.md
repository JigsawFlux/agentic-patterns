### 🥇 PRIMARY — Mercy General *(3.5 miles)*
**Receives:** All smoke inhalation, general trauma, and non-burn casualties.
**Rationale:** Closest trauma-certified facility with sufficient bed capacity (12 beds). Proximity is operationally critical given confirmed downtown traffic gridlock.

---

### 🥈 SECONDARY — St. Jude Medical *(7.2 miles)*
**Receives:** All burn-critical casualties and significant respiratory presentations requiring specialized care.
**Rationale:** The sole burn-capable facility in the network. Medically appropriate and protocol-mandated for all burn-critical patients regardless of distance.

> ⚠️ **CAPACITY ADVISORY:** St. Jude Medical has only **4 available burn beds**. EMS Sector Commander must monitor burn-critical patient volume in real time. **If burn casualty count approaches or exceeds 3 patients, initiate mutual aid hospital coordination immediately.**

---

### 🥉 TERTIARY — County Community *(5.0 miles — Overflow Only)*
**Receives:** Stable, non-critical patients only — mild smoke inhalation, minor lacerations, observation cases — if Mercy General approaches capacity.
**Restriction:** No critical or burn patients are to be routed to County Community under any circumstances.

---

- **St. Jude Medical Burn Unit** — Contact immediately upon scene arrival. Same notification requirements as above.
