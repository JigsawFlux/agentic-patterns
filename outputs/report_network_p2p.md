## 1. Incident Overview

A confirmed working structure fire is active at **45 Pine St (WC1A Bloomsbury, London)** — a 3-story commercial/residential building. Fire is tracking upward through the stairwell with upper floor extension confirmed as a live risk. Multiple smoke inhalation casualties are being actively extracted. Smoke composition remains **unconfirmed pending Hazmat assessment** — all agencies are operating under **chemical exposure protocol until cleared**.

Downtown midday conditions have produced peak foot and vehicle traffic gridlock. Wind is drifting **eastward toward Main St**, elevating passive civilian inhalation risk beyond the immediate structure perimeter.
