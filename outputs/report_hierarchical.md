## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

A 3-story building fire has been confirmed at 14 Kingsbourne Terrace, WC1B 9ZZ. Casualties include reported smoke inhalation victims and possible burns patients of unknown severity. Structural collapse and trauma injuries remain a live risk. All three emergency departments — Fire, Medical, and Police — have been activated and resources dispatched. A significant complicating factor is severe traffic gridlock on High Holborn, exacerbated by active road works near Holborn Circus, which poses a critical risk to response times across all services. Mitigation measures are underway. This report consolidates all departmental actions for command post situational awareness and ongoing coordination.

## SECTION 5 — COMMAND POST INSTRUCTIONS

The following directives are issued by the Incident Commander to all departments and the command post with immediate effect.

---

### 5.1 Immediate Priorities

> **1. MAINTAIN HIGH HOLBORN CORRIDOR**
> Police RPUs must sustain the cleared emergency vehicle corridor on High Holborn for the duration of the incident. This is the single most critical logistical factor affecting all department response times. The escort Response Car at the junction is to remain in position until the Incident Commander stands it down.

> **2. HART SCENE ASSESSMENT — PRIORITY REPORT**
> The HART Team is to transmit an initial casualty count and severity assessment to the command post as soon as the first triage sweep is complete. This report will determine whether MAJAX escalation is required at Northgate and whether additional medical resources must be drawn from reserve.

> **3. FIRE MUTUAL AID — INITIATE ASSESSMENT**
> Given that all available fire resources are committed, the command post is to immediately contact neighbouring fire stations to assess mutual aid availability. This is a precautionary measure; activation will be authorised by the Incident Commander upon receipt of the fire ground commander's structural assessment.

---

### 5.2 Escalation Triggers — Incident Commander Authorisation Required

| Trigger | Action Required |
|---|---|
| Casualty count exceeds current projections | Activate MAJAX protocol at Northgate; draw down RRV reserves |
| Structural collapse confirmed | Activate Fire mutual aid immediately; request Urban Search and Rescue (USAR) |
| Northgate Burns Unit capacity reached (4 beds) | Divert critical burns to next available specialist centre — command post to identify now |
| Crowd control exceeds Response Car capacity | Activate PSU Vans from standby |

| Road corridor fails or gridlock worsens | Authorise helicopter transport for critical casualties via Northgate helipad |

### 5.3 Inter-Department Coordination Requirements

- 🔥🚑 **Fire ↔ Medical:** Fire Incident Commander on scene to maintain direct radio contact with HART Team Leader. Casualty handover points to be formally established at the scene perimeter before the first patients are moved.

- 🚑🚔 **Medical ↔ Police:** Police escort car at the High Holborn junction to actively guide incoming DCA convoys. Medical Dispatch to notify Police of each DCA wave departure time so escort can be positioned accordingly.
